        A questionnaire for the assessment of disgust sensitivity

        Abstract.Background: Up until now there has been no questionnaire for the assessment of disgust sensitivity in the German language. Objective: Development of such an instrument. Method: A preliminary questionnaire was given to 235 subjects. Using the data a principal-axis solution with a subsequent oblique rotation was computed. The factor structure was confirmed through a confirmatory factor analysis (n = 501). Results: The questionnaire for the assessment of disgust sensitivity (FFE) consists of five scales: death, body secretions, hygiene, spoilage, oral rejection, and 37 items in total. Conclusions: This outcome points to the possible significance of disgust sensitivity for the development and maintenance of anxiety disorders such as blood phobias and obsessive compulsive disorders.

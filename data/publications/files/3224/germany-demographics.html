As of 2021, the population of Germany is projected at 83.90 million, up by 0.14% compared with 83.78 million in 2020. Germany is ranked 19th in terms of population and 191st in terms of population growth rate.

There are 0.79 million births in Germany in 2021. That is 2,165 per day, which is ranked 37th. The crude birth rate is 9.46 per 1000 people. There are 0.97 million deaths in Germany in 2021. That is 2,647 per day, which is ranked 10th. The crude death rate is 11.57 per 1000 people.

64.04 million people are living in urban areas, which is 77.54% of Germany population. Germany is ranked 77th in terms of urbanization.

The Sex Ratio in Germany in 2021 is 97.88 males per 100 females. There are 41.50 million males and 42.40 million females in Germany. The percentage of female population is 50.54% compare to 49.46% male population. Germany has 0.90 million more females than males. Germany is at 81st position out of 201 countries/territories in terms of female to male ratio.
